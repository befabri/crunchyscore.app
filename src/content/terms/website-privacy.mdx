# Website Privacy Policy

This privacy policy sets out the data processing practices specific to our website, [{frontmatter.website}](https://crunchyscore.app/).

## Information Collection, Use, and Sharing

**Website Analytics:** {frontmatter.website} uses Plausible Analytics to track overall trends in the usage of our website. Plausible Analytics collects only aggregated information, which does not allow us to identify any visitor to our website.
The purpose of collecting this information is to understand how our website is used and to improve our services accordingly. For more information about how Plausible Analytics handles data, please visit the [Plausible Analytics Data Policy](https://plausible.io/data-policy).

## Data Center Location

Our servers and data centers are located in Amsterdam, The Netherlands. All the data collected by Plausible is stored and processed in this location. Despite this, our services are not restricted to this geographical location. Users worldwide can access and use this website, but please be aware that your information will be processed and stored in accordance with the data protection laws and regulations applicable in The Netherlands and the European Union, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## Security

The security of your Personal Information is important to us, and we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect it. But remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

## Cookies

Our Website {frontmatter.name} does not use "cookies".

## Changes to this Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy from time to time. The most current version of the policy will govern our processing of your personal data and will always be available on this page.
